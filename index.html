Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect my IoT device to WiFi?

Navigate to device configuration, select + button, and follow the guided instructions. Ensure your device is in pairing mode and connected to a 2.4GHz network.

How can I reset my IoT device to factory settings?

Press and hold the reset button for 10-15 seconds until the LED indicator changes color or pattern. The device will return to factory settings and require reconfiguration.

Can I control multiple IoT devices simultaneously?

Absolutely! Our platform supports multi-device control. Create custom groups and automation scenes to manage multiple devices with single commands or schedules.
